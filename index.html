Self Care

Why is self care so important? Self care is the practice of taking an active role in protecting one's own well-being and happiness. Self-care is important to maintain a healthy relationship with yourself as it produces positive feelings and boost your self esteem.

Self care examples: meditation, healthy diet, vacation, yoga, exercise, coloring, and etc.Self care is personal so there are many different examples on what can help people be happy or at least improve hier well-being.
